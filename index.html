I am a non-tenured associate professor in the Intelligent Information Processing Laboratory (IIPL), School of Communication Engineering, Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU). Before joining HDU, I received my Ph.D. degree in the LIU Laboratory for SMART Animal Management (SmartAM), City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2023, and supervised by Prof. Kai Liu.

My research interests include animal activity recognition, sound recognition, wearable sensors, machine learning and deep learning.
